The Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) is a valuable resource for local communities and businesses seeking quality information using social science methods. CARIT provides high-quality survey research, program evaluation, performance measurement, and experimental research using scientifically rigorous information technology methods. The center is an interdisciplinary research center that collaborates with other disciplines to apply computing in new ways to solve challenging problems. CARIT assists faculty in research projects, grants, and contracts while also offering organizations and agencies technology evaluations and business analysis. CARIT also offers an applied learning environment for Kennesaw State University graduate and undergraduate students in all aspects of computing and its applications in engineering, science, and technology.
